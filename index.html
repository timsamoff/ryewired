Ryewired

Version 0.1.0

An audio circuit breadboard simulator for hobbyists. Drop components from the palette onto the board, wire holes together, then press Run to simulate.

Connect a Signal Generator and open the Oscilloscope or Spectrum Analyzer to see how your circuit shapes audio. Try "illegal" connections — the sim will tell you what went wrong.
